Welcome to the Curriculum Vitae of Alexander Jüstel
===================================================

This website contains the curriculum vitae of Alexander Jüstel, currently research assistant at the `Fraunhofer IEG <https://www.ieg.fraunhofer.de/>`_ in Aachen and PhD candidate at the `Geological Institute at RWTH Aachen University <https://www.geol.rwth-aachen.de/>`_.
The website contains a list of publications and conference contributions, information about the open-source contributions made, information about the professional and academic career as well as participations in workshops, summer schools, and conducted field work.
